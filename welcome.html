What is an Open Elective?

Open Electives are courses that allow students to explore subjects beyond their core curriculum. These courses help in broadening knowledge, developing new skills, and enhancing career prospects.

At JNTUA, students can choose from a variety of open electives spanning different domains such as Engineering, Management, Humanities, and Science.
